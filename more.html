Societies & Collaborations

Research Networks

Iberada Research Network

During my time at the Brain-Machine Interface Systems Lab at Miguel Hernández University of Elche (September 2012 – December 2015), I was an active collaborator of Iberada, an Ibero-American research network dedicated to the study and development of ICT applications based on interfaces adapted for people with disabilities.

Associate Project

Since January 2014, I collaborated on the Associate project: "Decoding and stimulation of motor and sensory brain activity to support long term potentiation through Hebbian and paired associative stimulation during rehabilitation of gait." The project investigates novel interventions for promoting motor control re-learning in neurological patients (CVA and spinal cord injury), combining motor planning at brain level, sensory stimulation, and wearable lower-limb exoskeletons.
